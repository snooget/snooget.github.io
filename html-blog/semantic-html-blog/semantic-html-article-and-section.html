Now that we have covered the head of semantic html, lets go over what happens in the body.
The two elements we will focus on in this page are section and article.

Section defines elements in a document, such as chapters, headings, or any area of the document with the
same theme. For example, articles covering the sport cricket could go under a single section
while a websites homepage could be split into sections for the introduction, news items, and contact information.
