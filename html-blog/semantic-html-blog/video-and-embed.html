Video and Embed

As demonstrated in the previous slide, media content can be a useful addition to a website. By using a video element, we can add videos to our website. The video element makes it clear that a developer is attempting to display a video to the user. Here are three big attributes that affect the video's playback:

  • controls: When added in, a play and pause button will be added in the video. A volume control and fullscreen option are also avaliable with this attribute.
  • autoplay: This attribute results in the video automatically being played.
  • loop: This attribute will result in the video being payed on repeat.

Another tag that can be used to incorporate media on a webpage is the embed tag, which can embed any media files from souces such as audio foles, videos, and gifs. This means that websites that have an embed button have some form of media content thatc can be added to other websites.

The "embed" tag is a self-closing tag unlike the video element. Note that "embed" is a depreciated tag and other alternatives should be used. such as video, audio, and img.
